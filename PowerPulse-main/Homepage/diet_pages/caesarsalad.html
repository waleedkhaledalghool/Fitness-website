Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Add the Caesar dressing and toss to coat.
  3. Top with sliced grilled chicken if desired.
  4. Serve immediately.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 Calories: 220 kcal (without chicken)
