Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 cup kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the fruit.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lime juice.
  3. Drizzle the honey-lime dressing over the fruit and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 Calories: 120 kcal
