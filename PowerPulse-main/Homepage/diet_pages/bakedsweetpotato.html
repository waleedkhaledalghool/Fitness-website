Baked Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash the sweet potatoes very well.
  3. Cut the sweet potatoes into wedges or into the desired shape.
  4. Toss the wedges in olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Place the wedges on a baking sheet and Bake for 25-30 minutes, until tender and golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Nutrition

Serving: 1 Calories: 190 kcal
