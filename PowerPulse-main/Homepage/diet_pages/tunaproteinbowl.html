Tuna Protein Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, add the steamed rice ,top with tuna, quinoa, and cucumber.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 Calories: 350 kcal
