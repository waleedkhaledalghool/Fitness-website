Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 tbsp ranch dressing

Instructions

  1. Place the sliced chicken in the center of the tortilla.
  2. Top with lettuce, and cheese.
  3. Drizzle with ranch dressing.
  4. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll up tightly.
  5. Cut in half and serve.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 wrap Calories: 380 kcal
