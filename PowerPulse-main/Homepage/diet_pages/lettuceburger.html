Lettuce Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 large lettuce leaves
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 slice of onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grill the beef patty until it reaches your desired doneness.
  2. Place one lettuce leaf on a plate.
  3. Top with the beef patty, cheese, tomato, onion, mayonnaise, and mustard.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cover with the second lettuce leaf and serve.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 burger Calories: 320 kcal
