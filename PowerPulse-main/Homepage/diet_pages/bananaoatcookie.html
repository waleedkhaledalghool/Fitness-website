Banana Oat Cookie

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  3. Stir in the oats, chocolate chips, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.

Nutrition

Serving: 1 cookie Calories: 80 kcal
